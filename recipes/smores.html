Campfire smores

HOME

Description

These s'mores cones are neater and easier to make than traditional s'mores. We love making them while camping or sitting around the fire pit. The quantity can easily be adjusted to suit your needs.

Ingredients

  • 4 large waffle cones
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter chips
  • 1/2 cup butterscotch chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill or campfire to medium heat.
  2. Place 1/4 cup of mini marshmallows in each waffle cone.
  3. Add 1/4 cup of chocolate chips, followed by 2 tablespoons of peanut butter chips and butterscotch chips.
  4. If using, sprinkle chopped nuts on top.
  5. Wrap each cone in aluminum foil, ensuring they are tightly sealed.
  6. Place the wrapped cones on the grill or near the campfire for about 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally until the chocolate and marshmallows are melted.
  7. Carefully remove the cones from the heat, unwrap them, and enjoy your delicious s'mores!
